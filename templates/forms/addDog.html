Dog Information Form

Dogs representing CCC are required to obtain an American Kennel Club (AKC) Canine Good Citizen Certificate. Please refer to the AKC Website for more information on the requirements and how to obtain this certificate. CCC also encourages handlers to strive toward AKC's Community Canine Certificate. Many pet stores (such as PetSmart and Petco) offer AKC testing at very reasonable rates.

CCC requires a current Health Certificate to be completed prior to volunteering. This Health Certificate needs to be updated annually by the dog's veteranarian. The Health Certificate form is located here, and is also available on the main forms page of the site.

Medical Certificate

CCC requires that a medical certificate be on file for ALL dogs utilized as a part of this ministry. This medical certificate ensures us that your dog has had its yearly vet visit and all vaccinations are up to date. We require this certificate to be filled out and sent to us annually.
The medical certificate can be found on our forms page and is available as a PDF. Please take this to your vet and have them fill it out and sign it. You can then scan and email it to us for our records.
